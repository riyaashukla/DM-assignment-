3. Short Explanation
a. What type of content did you create?
I created a blog article that analyzes recent trends in digital marketing and explains how these strategies affect consumer behavior.
b. Why is it original?
The article is original because I personally researched the topic, wrote all the content in my own words, and provided unique examples not copied from elsewhere.
c. How does copyright protect it?
Copyright automatically protects my article by giving me the exclusive right to use, share, and publish it. Others cannot copy or use my work without permission.
d. If someone copied your work, how would you handle it?
I would first request that the person remove the copied content or credit me. If they do not comply, I would report the issue to the hosting platform or take legal action to enforce my rights.
