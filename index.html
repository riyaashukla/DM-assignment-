Digital Marketing Funnel

From Awareness to Conversion

1
Awareness
Reach potential customers through ads, social media, and SEO to make them aware of your brand.
2
Interest
Engage with valuable content, blog posts, and newsletters to spark interest in your products or services.
3
Consideration
Provide demos, case studies, and testimonials to help prospects evaluate your offerings.
4
Intent
Encourage potential buyers to take action with targeted offers and personal communication.
5
Conversion
Convert leads into customers by completing purchases and providing smooth checkout experiences.
6
Loyalty
Build lasting relationships with follow-ups, loyalty programs, and continued engagement.
